## Account and Access

<ExpandableGroup>

<Expandable title="How do I create an account?">

Follow these steps to set up your account quickly.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit Signup" icon="user-plus">
    Go to the Cheney Communications homepage and click "Sign Up".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Details" icon="edit-3">
    Provide your email, name, and password. Verify with the sent code.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm" icon="check-circle">
    Check your inbox and activate your account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

</Expandable>

<Expandable title="What if I forgot my password?">

Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your registered email to receive a reset link valid for 15 minutes. Create a strong password with at least 12 characters, including uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols.

</Expandable>

<Expandable title="Can I use multiple accounts?">

You manage one primary account per email. For teams, invite members via the dashboard after logging in. Upgrade to pro plans for unlimited sub-accounts.

</Expandable>

</ExpandableGroup>

## Beta Participation

<Expandable title="How to join the beta program?" default-open="true">

Sign up now to test upcoming features.

1. Visit `/beta-signup`.
2. Enter your details.
3. Await approval email (1-2 days).

Once approved, configure your beta environment:

<CodeGroup tabs="Node.js,Python">
  ```javascript
  const cheney = require('cheney-comm');
  const client = new cheney.Client({ apiKey: 'YOUR_BETA_KEY' });
  client.connect();
  ```
  ```python
  import cheney
  client = cheney.Client(api_key="YOUR_BETA_KEY")
  client.connect()
  ```
</CodeGroup>

Report issues via the beta dashboard.

</Expandable>

<Expandable title="What are beta limitations?">

Beta users experience occasional downtime for updates. Features may change before launch. Use production keys only after full release.

</Expandable>
