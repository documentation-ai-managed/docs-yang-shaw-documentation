## Quick Start

Get up and running with Cheney Communications in minutes. Follow these steps to create your account, explore the dashboard, and send your first message. The platform provides secure messaging, team collaboration, and real-time notifications tailored for modern teams.

<Callout kind="tip">
  Prepare your email address and a strong password. Verification happens instantly via email.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Visit [cheneycommunications.com/signup](https://cheneycommunications.com/signup) and enter your details.

    - Email: Use a work email like `you@yourcompany.com`
    - Password: At least 12 characters with uppercase, numbers, and symbols

    Click **Create Account** to proceed.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for the verification email from Cheney Communications.

    Click the link to activate your account. If you don't see it, check spam.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Up Profile" icon="settings">
    Log in and complete your profile:

    - Add your name and photo
    - Select your team or department
    - Enable two-factor authentication (recommended)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Navigation Tips

Navigate efficiently across devices.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Desktop" icon="monitor">
    Use the left sidebar for channels and the right for details.

    Keyboard shortcuts speed things up:

    ````bash
    Ctrl/Cmd + K     # Quick switcher
    Ctrl/Cmd + Shift + L  # Open notifications
    Ctrl/Cmd + /     # Search messages
    ````

    Pin your most-used channels to the top.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Mobile" icon="smartphone">
    Swipe left for channels, right for threads.

    Tap the compose button (`+`) at bottom-right to start new messages.

    Enable push notifications in **Settings > Notifications**.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Beta Features

Access cutting-edge features still in testing.

<ExpandableGroup>
  <Expandable title="Enable Real-Time Voice Channels" default-open="true">
    Beta voice channels let you jump into audio discussions instantly.

    1. Go to **Settings > Beta Features**
    2. Toggle **Voice Channels** on
    3. Join a channel and click the microphone icon

    <Callout kind="alert">
      Voice is limited to 10 participants per call in beta. Provide feedback via `/feedback` slash command.
    </Callout>
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="AI Message Summaries">
    Get automatic summaries of long threads.

    Enable in **Settings > Labs > AI Tools**. Summaries appear above threads longer than 50 messages.
  </Expandable>
</ExpandableGroup>
